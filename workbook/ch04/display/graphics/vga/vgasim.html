How VGA Works

Horizontal Scan: The electron beam scans from left to right, drawing one line of pixels.

Blanking Intervals: During sync and porch periods, the beam is turned off (blank) while repositioning.

Sync Pulses: H-Sync moves beam to next line, V-Sync returns to top of screen.

Pixel Clock: Each pixel is output at the pixel clock frequency (25.175 MHz for 640x480@60Hz).
